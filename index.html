Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • Thanks to thick pads of skin on their chest and knees, camels can comfortably sit in very hot sand.

Imteresting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication.
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from when Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first Teddy bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • Teddy Bears are one of the most popular toys ever.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are bears.
  • Pandas are also very famous for being stuffed toys.
  • You can find most Pandas in Zoos.

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas are very interesting insects.
  • They are able to fly.
  • Cicadas are herbivores.
